HOMIE

Homie is an Australian label that was created to address youth homelessness in Melbourne. They have been taking steps towards demanding greater transparency, sustainability and safer textile workplaces and practices within the fashion industry. Throughout the process of looking into alternate, ethical suppliers have allowed them to strengthen this aspect of their business while ensuring high quality garments, along with strong ethical standards.
